My career

I have about six years of working experience in China and a half year of working experience in Japan as a Senior Software Engineer. During these six years, I have earned developing skills including iOS/Android development, windows desktop application development, website backend, and frontend development. Recently, I'm focusing on Machine Learning researching guided by one of my professors.
